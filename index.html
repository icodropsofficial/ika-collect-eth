▶ How to use

This tool helps you to collect Ethereum tokens from many Ethereum wallets to a single one.

Enter the address you want to send tokens to. If balance is displayed, it is valid. After that, enter the private keys for wallets you want to send tokens from (all data is processed in your browser, we DO NOT HAVE ACCESS TO YOUR KEYS). They'll get replaced with the wallets' addresses.

Finally, insert the gas price and how many ETH you want to send. Accept ToS and press 'fire!'. Wait for clock icons to get replaced with transactions' status. You can hover over a cross to see why the transaction has failed, or click the successful transaction icon to see the transaction on Etherscan. Note that Etherscan sees if a transaction is confirmed a bit later, so don't get scared if you see a green tick and 'Pending' on Etherscan.

In some (very rare) cases a transaction may get confirmed, but then fall out of the main chain if a fork occurs, so a tick means that a transaction will almost definitely stay in the chain.
